Masks Wearing Practices & Social Media Sentiment

Our Mask/Sentiment Dashboard provides invaluable insight into mask wearing practices and COVID-19 social sentiment trends across the United States, focusing on first wave of the virus, during the time period between January-August 2020. This dashboard uses data gathered from 1) a sample of 14.8 billion unique tweets from 52 million Twitter users filtered and scored using the VADER sentiment analysis tool, and 2) a nation-wide survey of over 250,000 individuals asking the question, "How often do you wear a mask in public when you expect to be within six feet of another person?"

Forecasting Cases & ICU Bed Shortages Across States

By 1) drawing data from every hospital in every state across the United States, and 2) analyzing rolling differences to build forecasts, our Cases/ICU Beds Dashboard provides invaluable inight into the spread of COVID-19 and warning signs for depleted hospital resources.

Tracking Vaccination Rates Across States

By compiling vaccination data from every county in every state across the United States, our Vaccinations Dashboard provides invaluable insight into the country's ongoing fight against the virus. In particular, this dashboard details which states are leading the way in vaccinations, and which states are lagging behind, as of March 8, 2021.
